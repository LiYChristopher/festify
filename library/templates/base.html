Welcome to Spotifest

Spotify account required. Create a Spotify account

In Jukebox-style, we help you create a Spotify playlist based on your unique taste within 2 minutes! Make a playlist for yourself, share that playlist with friends, or make one together with friends.

Creating a playlist

To create a new playlist, click "create festival". If a friend shared a festival ID with you, you can enter the ID instead under "join festival".

By analyzing the artists you follow on Spotify, and those you have in your Spotify playlists, we carefully and ingeniously create your unique taste profile. No taste is the same! Once carefully crafted, we'll guide you through to the Festival page, where you can further customize what you are in the mood for:

Finetuning preferences for a festival

Whether you joined a festival or started one by yourself, you can now add more artists to the taste-profile mix, adjust various parameters to suit your current mood, and click generate / propose vision when you're set!

Joint Festivals

If you're still waiting for friends to join, share the festival ID with them (see image below), and refresh the page once they've joined. As you can see below, once they are set you will know!

All contributors' artists will be combined, and your parameters averaged to truly create a festival that belongs to you all.

Solo or together, your generated playlist is sure to provide you an interesting playlist for you to enjoy and share!
